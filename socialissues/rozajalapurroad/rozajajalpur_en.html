Who is Responsible?

The sole responsible body for all infrastructure, including roads, in Greater Noida West is the Greater Noida Industrial Development Authority (GNIDA).

Step 4: Legal Options (Last Resort)

If no administrative measures work, you have legal rights. It is advisable to consult a lawyer for this.

This is the first legal step. Send a formal notice to GNIDA through a lawyer, detailing the problem and giving a timeline for action.

If an accident or property damage has occurred due to the bad road, you can file a civil suit for compensation. This requires proving negligence.

You can file a Writ of 'Mandamus' in the High Court to order GNIDA to fulfill its legal duty (road maintenance).
