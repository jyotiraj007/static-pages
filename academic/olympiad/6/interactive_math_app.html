Welcome to Your Interactive Study Hub!

This guide is designed to transform your Maths Olympiad preparation from a static reading list into an engaging, hands-on experience. The Olympiad challenges you to think critically and apply mathematical concepts in clever ways. Here, you'll find a structured path to build your skills, practice with targeted questions, and gain the confidence you need for exam day.

What This Guide Offers:

  • A curated list of the **best study materials** for a strong foundation.
  • A visual **topic weightage map** to help you focus your efforts.
  • An interactive **Practice Zone** with topic-wise question sets.
  • Instant feedback and **step-by-step solutions** for every question.

How to Get Started:

  1. Visit the **Study Plan** tab to understand the syllabus and key resources.
  2. Dive into the **Practice Zone** to test your knowledge on specific topics.
  3. Review solutions carefully to learn new problem-solving techniques.
  4. Practice consistently to build speed and accuracy. Good luck!
